World Hackathon Olympiad (WHO)

The World Hackathon Olympiad challenges aspiring programmers to develop innovative software solutions within a time limit. Participants solve coding problems, build applications, and collaborate on projects.

Age Groups:

  • Class 3rd to 5th
  • Class 6th to 8th
