World Junior Science Olympiad (WJSO)

The World Junior Science Olympiad is a challenging competition for young minds passionate about science. This Olympiad covers physics, chemistry, and biology with a difficulty level higher than the general Science Olympiad.

Age Groups:

  • Class 3rd to 5th
  • Class 6th to 8th
  • Class 9th to 12th
