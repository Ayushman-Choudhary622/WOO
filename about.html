About WOO '25

Welcome to the World Organisation for Olympiads (WOO)! We aim to provide a platform for students to challenge themselves, enhance their knowledge, and gain recognition through various Olympiads.

At WOO, we host:

  • World Mathematics Olympiad (WMO)
  • World Science Olympiad (WSO)
  • World Chemistry Olympiad (WCO)
  • World Physics Olympiad (WPO)
  • World Biology Olympiad (WBO)
  • World Junior Science Olympiad (WJSO)
  • World Reasoning Olympiad (WRO)
  • World Hackathon Olympiad (WHO)

Our Olympiads are free to participate in, with digital certificates and honorable mentions for outstanding performers. Results will be declared class-wise across three age groups.
